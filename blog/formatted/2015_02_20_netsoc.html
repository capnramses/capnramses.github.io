WebGL Talk for Internet Society

I did a talk this wednesday night (18th Feb 2015) for the Dublin University Internet Society - "netsoc". You can find my slides here. The idea was to do an introduction to WebGL for students that have a strong web and Internet background, but not necessarily graphics programming experience. My main theme or point was to contrast why previous attempts at on-line 3d didn't really take off against some of the advantages of webgl. I also had a little code example of getting started.
