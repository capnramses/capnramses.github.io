Maths Textbooks

I asked in a tweet what everyone's favourite 3d maths textbooks/resources were for learning. Here is a summary of the responses. I ordered two of these books, and I have Lengyel's earlier book and a Graphics Codex membership, so I should be able to do a comparative review soon too.
  • John Vince, Mathematics for Computer Graphics
  • J. Ström, K. Åström, and T. Akenine-Möller, immersivemath.com (online)
  • Eric Lengyel, Foundations of Game Engine Development, Volume 1: Mathematics
  • Eric Lengyel, Mathematics for 3D Game Programming and Computer Graphics, Third Edition
  • Khan Academy, https://www.khanacademy.org/math/linear-algebra (online)
  • Morgan McGuire, graphicscodex.com (online)
